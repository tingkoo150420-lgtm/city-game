## Features

### City World

- Large open world map
- Randomly generated buildings
- Sandbox style gameplay

### NPC System

Two types of NPCs exist in the world.

Civilian NPCs  
- Wander randomly around the city

Police NPCs  
- Chase the player when the wanted level increases
- Can damage the player when close

### Vehicles

- Cars are placed around the map
- The player can enter and exit vehicles
- Police cars can chase the player

### Combat System

- The player can shoot using the mouse
- Bullets damage NPCs
- NPCs have health and can be defeated

### Wanted System

Police behavior changes depending on the wanted level.

Example escalation:

Level 1–2  
Police chase the player

Level 3  
Police vehicles begin chasing

Level 4  
A helicopter begins pursuing the player

### Weapon Shop

A simple weapon shop system is included.

Available weapons:

- Rifle
- Shotgun
- Sniper
